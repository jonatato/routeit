# Landing Page Wireframe (v2)

Goal: highlight new notifications, Today view, reminders, and collaborative planning.

## Structure

1) Top Nav
- Logo + product name
- Links: Features, How it works, Pricing, Login
- Primary CTA: "Create itinerary"

2) Hero
- Headline: "Your trip, on time and on track"
- Subhead: "Plan days, track tasks, and get reminders before departure"
- CTA row: Primary "Start planning" + Secondary "See demo"
- Hero visual: split card with "Today" view + notifications tray

3) Social proof
- Short stat row: trips planned, teams using, avg time saved

4) Feature grid (new highlights)
- Today View: "See what happens today and do not miss tasks"
- Smart reminders: "7-day email checklist"
- Notifications: "In-app alerts for itinerary updates"
- Collaborative planning: "Editors + viewers"

5) How it works
- Step 1: Create itinerary
- Step 2: Add days + checklist
- Step 3: Share + get reminders

6) Visual section: Today View
- Wireframe block showing day card + list of tasks + map preview
- CTA: "Open Today view"

7) Visual section: Notifications
- Bell tray mockup with alert items
- Copy: "Never miss changes or deadlines"

8) Checklist section
- Two-column: Itinerary checklist + Personal bag checklist
- Copy: "Both are combined in reminders"

9) Pricing teaser
- Free plan highlights + button to pricing

10) Footer
- Links: Privacy, Terms, Support, Contact

## Layout Notes
- Keep hero above the fold.
- Use a clean 2-column hero with a tall visual card.
- Feature grid: 2x2 on desktop, stacked on mobile.
- Use a light background with subtle gradient and a warm accent.

## CTAs
- Primary: "Start planning"
- Secondary: "View pricing"
- Tertiary: "Open Today view"
